## What It Does

Tierlist allows you to create structured tierlists where items can be ranked across multiple dimensions:

- **Templates**: Create reusable tierlist templates with custom tiers (rows), categories (columns), and cards (items)
- **Rankings**: Fill out templates by dragging cards into cells to create your personal ranking
- **Sharing**: Share your rankings with view-only or editable links, allowing others to see or collaborate on rankings

## Key Features

- **Categorized Tierlists**: Unlike traditional tierlists, items can be placed in a grid with both tiers (rows) and categories (columns)
- **Discord Authentication**: Log in with Discord to save your templates and rankings
- **Public Templates**: Make templates public for others to use and rank
- **Copy Templates**: Copy public templates to customize them for your own use
- **Share Rankings**: Share view-only or editable links to your rankings
- **Co-owners**: Editable links allow others to become co-owners and collaborate
- **Auto-save**: Rankings are automatically saved as you make changes
- **Share as Image**: Export your ranking as an image to share elsewhere
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Comfortable viewing in any environment with system theme detection
- **Multilingual**: Available in English and German

## How to Use

1. **Create a Template**: Start by creating a new template with your desired tiers, columns, and cards
2. **Start Ranking**: Click "Start Ranking" on any template to create your personal ranking
3. **Drag Cards**: Drag cards from the unranked area into the grid cells to rank them
4. **Share**: Use the Share button to get links for others to view or edit your ranking
